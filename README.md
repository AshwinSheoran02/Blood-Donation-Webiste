# Blood-Donation-Webiste
Blood Donation Webiste created for GDSC workshop

Access the website here

https://Blood-Donation-Webiste.ashwinsheoran.repl.co   (Website hosted on replit)

or here 

https://ashwinsheoran02.github.io/Blood-Donation-Webiste/

## Problem Statement and Solution
People find it difficult to donate blood if they want to; they either have to go to a nearby hospital or wait for a blood donation camp to happen somewhere near.Our website helps people donate blood right from the comfort of their homes, where users can book appointments, and our volunteers can come to the home to collect their blood for donation.
